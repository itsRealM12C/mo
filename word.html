Welcome to Word

This is a fully-featured word processor interface built entirely with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It mimics the look and behavior of a desktop office suite, running right in your browser.

Try These Features

You can bold, italicize, underline, or color any text by selecting it and clicking the buttons in the ribbon above. Try changing the font or size of this paragraph.

The ribbon tabs across the top — Home, Insert, Layout, References, Review, View — all contain functional groups of tools, just like the real application.

Editing Tips

  • Right-click anywhere in this document for a context menu
  • Use Ctrl+B, Ctrl+I, Ctrl+U for quick formatting
  • Use Ctrl+Z and Ctrl+Y for undo and redo
  • Press Ctrl+F to open the find bar
  • Use the zoom slider in the bottom-right corner
  • Click the File tab to open the backstage view

Style your text using the Styles gallery in the Home tab. Choose from Title, Headings, Subtitle, Quote, and more. The word count at the bottom of the screen updates live as you type.

Word processors are among the most enduring applications in personal computing — their core interaction model has remained remarkably stable for forty years.
